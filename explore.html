About Vazhi

This website is designed to help students and parents explore and understand a wide range of career opportunities beyond the conventional focus on a few highly competitive entrance exams such as JEE and NEET. Our goal is to provide clear insights into various courses, entrance exams, and educational pathways, enabling students to analyse their options and make informed career decisions.

We focus exclusively on government-funded and merit-based institutions — Central, State, Aided, and Deemed — that offer affordable, high-quality higher education. These institutions have established infrastructure, experienced faculty, and strong alumni networks.

Rather than limiting choices to a few well-known pathways, this platform aims to present a broader spectrum of courses and opportunities — offering a "buffet" of career choices that students can explore based on their interests, aspirations, and strengths.

Central Govt Scholarships
  • KVPY — ₹5,000–7,000/month for science students. Conducted by IISc. Highly competitive but life-changing.
  • INSPIRE SHE (DST) — ₹80,000/year for B.Sc / Integrated M.Sc in natural sciences.
  • NSP (National Scholarship Portal) — OBC, SC, ST, minority, PwD scholarships. Apply at scholarships.gov.in
  • AICTE Pragati & Saksham — ₹50,000/year for girl students and students with disabilities in technical courses.
🎓
IIT & NIT Fee Waiver
  • Family income < ₹1 LPA → Full fee waiver (₹0 tuition at IITs/NITs)
  • Family income ₹1–5 LPA → 2/3rd fee waiver
  • SC/ST students → additional benefits + hostel support at most central institutes
  • This means a student from a low-income family can study at IIT for nearly FREE.
🌟
Tamil Nadu State Schemes
  • Chief Minister's Scholarship (BC/MBC) — ₹1,000–3,000/month for higher education
  • SC/ST scholarship — full fee reimbursement at govt & aided colleges
  • First Graduate Scheme — additional support for families sending first child to college
  • TN 7.5% reservation — horizontal reservation for govt school students in engineering, medicine, law, agriculture
🔄
Missed the Exam? Backup Routes
  • Engineering: Miss JEE Main → Apply via TNEA for TN engineering colleges
  • Engineering alternate: Polytechnic Diploma (3 yrs) → B.Tech Lateral Entry (2 yrs)
  • Medical: Miss NEET → Nursing, Pharmacy, Physiotherapy, B.Sc Allied Health via state exams
  • Law: Miss CLAT → Many state law colleges have their own entrance or merit-based admissions
  • After graduation: GATE score → M.Tech at IITs/NITs + PSU jobs (government companies)
